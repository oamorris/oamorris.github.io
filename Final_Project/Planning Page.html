The best way to plan a road trip is to break down the planning process into simple and easy to follow steps.

Planning for a road trip can be both exciting and overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be difficult. Let me help you take away some of that road trip planning stress.

If you plan to road trip with others, make sure they are involved in the decision making process as well.

  • Itinerary – What region do you want to travel?

  • Timeframe – How long will the road trip be?

  • Weather – What climates will you experience?

  • Vehicle – How are you going to get around? Car, SUV or Campervan?

  • Camping / Sleeping Options – Where do you plan to sleep? Hotels, campsites or maybe in your car?

  • Food – Will you cook your own food or eat at local restaurants? What kind of road trip snack will you pack?

  • Must- See Experiences – Is there an attraction or site you really want to see?

  • Age: Life is about experiencing it all big or small, All ages is Welcome to enjoy!!
