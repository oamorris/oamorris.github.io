Who'd ever think we would get a chance to ride a horse!

Location: Mountains of North Carolina- Sandy Bottom Trail Rides (1459 Caney Fork Rd, Marshall, NC 28753-5476)

This was such a beautful Trip, we could go on and on about how much fun we had just and learning so much about the two beautiful horses we rode!

We meaning "My Fiance' and Myself" took this trip in mid-October 2022 and words couldnt describe the fun we had! This was more of a Romantic Date that I prayed didn't go wrong, since this was a surprise for him. We have never rode a horse before, better yet been so close to one and we decided to take them up a mountain! Yes! I agree, What were we thinking??

But it was unmarkly beautiful, the air was the Freshest air to smell, felt so clean and it just took our breathes away. This trip can be taking year round, with children or without! there are private tours, beach tours if the mountains scare you. Its fun for any and everyone. If you feel you cant climb a horse, dont feel bad "We both needed help". Also, a plus for us was that we actually felt like it was just us two riding horses, our tour guide Mike :) gave us the space we needed.
